# Cognitive & Grounding Exercises

## For Looping (The "Podcast in Your Head")

**Technique: Tactile Grounding**

Keep a small smooth stone in your pocket. When the loop starts:

1. Touch the stone
2. Say aloud: *"Touch. Now. Stone."*
3. Describe 3 things you can physically feel

*Why it works:* Touch bypasses the prefrontal cortex and resets the amygdala's fear circuit.

## For Memory Fading (Names & Terms)

**Technique: Photo-Associative Memory**

1. When meeting someone, look at their face for 3 full seconds
2. Create a vivid visual image linking their name to something (e.g., John → Johnny Appleseed)
3. Repeat their name twice in the first conversation

*Why it works:* Visual images encode in the hippocampus more reliably than auditory names alone.

## For Energy Crashes

**Technique: Power Nap Protocol**

1. Set timer for 20 minutes — no more
2. Lie down in a dark room
3. Do not enter deep sleep
4. Upon waking, drink cold water immediately

*Why it works:* 20 mins resets adrenaline without triggering sleep inertia.

## For Social Anxiety

**Technique: The 3-Second Rule**

1. Before entering a social situation, take 3 slow breaths
2. On the first exhale, relax your jaw
3. On the second, drop your shoulders
4. On the third, smile slightly

*Why it works:* This activates the parasympathetic nervous system and signals safety to the amygdala.
