# Nutrient Protocol

## Category A: Memory — Hippocampus Repair

| Nutrient | Dosage | Timing | Mechanism |
|----------|--------|--------|-----------|
| Alpha-GPC | 300mg | Morning + Afternoon | Direct acetylcholine donor, working memory |
| Lecithin (Phosphatidylcholine) | 1,000mg | Dinner | Brain cell membrane structure |
| Omega-3 DHA | 2g | With meals | Repairs neuronal membranes |
| B-Complex (high potency) | 1 cap | Breakfast | Nerve energy metabolism |

## Category B: Stability — Amygdala/Hypothalamus Repair

| Nutrient | Dosage | Timing | Mechanism |
|----------|--------|--------|-----------|
| Magnesium Threonate | 200mg | Before sleep | Crosses blood-brain barrier, GABA support |
| Ashwagandha (KSM-66) | 300mg | Evening | Lowers cortisol, stops looping |
| L-Theanine | 200mg | Before social/meetings | Calm alertness without sedation |
| Vitamin D3 + K2 | 5,000 IU | Morning with fat | Neuro-immune regulation |

## Category C: Energy — Mitochondrial Repair

| Nutrient | Dosage | Timing | Mechanism |
|----------|--------|--------|-----------|
| CoQ10 (Ubiquinone) | 200mg | Morning | Mitochondrial ATP production |
| Creatine Monohydrate | 5g | Anytime | Brain fuel, reduces mental fatigue |

## Stack Summary

```
Morning:    Alpha-GPC + B-Complex + D3+K2 + CoQ10 + Creatine
Afternoon:  Alpha-GPC
Evening:    Ashwagandha + Lecithin
Night:      Magnesium Threonate
PRN:        L-Theanine (before stressful events)
```

## Notes

- Take fat-soluble vitamins (D3, K2, CoQ10, Omega-3) **with food containing fat**
- Magnesium Threonate is preferred over other forms — it crosses the BBB effectively
- Cycle Ashwagandha: 6 weeks on, 1 week off
- Stay hydrated — neural function depends on electrolyte balance
