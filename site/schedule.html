Daily Recovery Schedule

A rhythm designed to stabilize your nervous system, rebuild neural pathways, and reduce anxiety, brain fog, and energy crashes.

Morning — Activation Phase

Morning recovery schedule
Time Action Purpose
7:00 AM 10 mins direct sunlight (eyes open) Reset circadian rhythm, wake prefrontal cortex
7:10 AM 500ml water + ½ tsp Himalayan salt Stabilize electrolytes for neuron firing
7:20 AM 10 min cold shower (10–15°C) Shock noradrenaline, increase focus without anxiety
7:40 AM High-protein breakfast (eggs, fish, spinach) Omega-3 + Choline for acetylcholine production

Midday — Cognitive Training

Midday recovery schedule
Time Action Purpose
12:00 PM 15 min memory drill — write 5 names/words, repeat Force hippocampal engagement
1:00 PM Low-glycemic lunch Stable insulin = stable energy, no crashes
3:00 PM Dopamine reset — walk outside, no phone Reduce mental looping, reset reward pathways

Evening — Recovery Phase

Evening recovery schedule
Time Action Purpose
8:00 PM Digital sunset — dim lights / blue filter Protect melatonin, enable REM sleep for memory cleaning
9:00 PM Grounding (5-4-3-2-1 method) Calm amygdala, stop nervous/fear loops
10:00 PM Nutrient dose (Magnesium + Melatonin) Neuron repair during sleep
10:30 PM Sleep in dark room (no screens) Brain defragmentation
