Why These Games Work

Depression is characterized by anhedonia, low motivation, and negative cognitive bias. Behavioral activation is one of the most effective evidence-based interventions for depression. Mood-tracking and accomplishment-based games provide small, repeatable wins that stimulate dopamine release and rebuild the brain's reward circuitry. Gratitude exercises have been shown to increase activity in the medial prefrontal cortex and reduce rumination.
