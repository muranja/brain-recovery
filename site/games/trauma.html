Why These Games Work

Trauma dysregulates the autonomic nervous system, keeping the brain in a chronic state of hyperarousal or hypoarousal. Safety-signaling games work by repeatedly orienting the brain to the present moment and the absence of threat. Grounding exercises activate the prefrontal cortex and inhibit amygdala hyperreactivity. Somatic awareness tools help re-establish interoceptive safety, which is often disrupted in PTSD.
