How N-Back Works

In dual n-back, you track two streams: a spatial position on a 3x3 grid and a spoken letter (A-H). For each trial, you must indicate whether the current position and/or letter matches the one from N steps ago. This forces your working memory to continuously update and maintain information. Research suggests dual n-back training can improve fluid intelligence and working memory capacity through strengthened prefrontal-parietal connectivity.
