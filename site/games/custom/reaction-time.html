Population Norms

Average simple reaction time: 200-250ms (young adults), 250-300ms (middle age), 300-400ms (older adults). Reaction time is affected by sleep, fatigue, caffeine, and neurological state. Consistent slowing may indicate cognitive fatigue or need for recovery. This test measures processing speed and vigilance attention — key components of cognitive function that improve with practice and adequate rest.
