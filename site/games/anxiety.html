Why These Games Work

Anxiety and panic disorders involve dysregulation of the autonomic nervous system, particularly the sympathetic (fight-or-flight) branch. Games that focus on slow, rhythmic breathing, sensory grounding, and progressive relaxation directly activate the parasympathetic nervous system via the vagus nerve. Research shows that even a single session of paced breathing can reduce state anxiety by up to 30%.
