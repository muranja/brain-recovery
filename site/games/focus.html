Why These Games Work

ADHD is characterized by impaired executive function, particularly in sustained attention, working memory, and impulse control. Structured interval techniques like Pomodoro leverage the brain's natural attention tunneling by providing clear time boundaries and frequent rewards. Gamified focus tools increase dopamine availability, which is often deficient in ADHD brains, making task initiation and continuation more achievable.
