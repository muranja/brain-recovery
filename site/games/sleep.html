Why These Games Work

Sleep onset depends on the parasympathetic nervous system's ability to activate and reduce cortical arousal. Slow breathing techniques like 4-7-8 directly stimulate the vagus nerve, lowering heart rate and blood pressure. Body scan and progressive relaxation exercises reduce cognitive hyperarousal, a primary driver of insomnia in neurological recovery. Consistent wind-down routines also strengthen circadian entrainment, improving overall sleep architecture.
