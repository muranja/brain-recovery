About NeuroReboot

This site exists because mental health recovery is poorly understood and deeply isolating. The protocols here were built from personal experience, published research, and direct experimentation across anxiety, depression, trauma, and neurological conditions. They are shared in the hope that others might find a faster path to stability than the one we walked alone.

Always consult a qualified healthcare practitioner before starting any supplement regimen or recovery protocol.
