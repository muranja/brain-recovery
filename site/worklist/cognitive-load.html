    Why This Helps

    Reducing cognitive load allows the prefrontal cortex to process information more effectively. When you break text into smaller chunks or extract only the essential points, your brain doesn't have to filter out noise — it can focus on understanding. This is especially helpful for ADHD, brain fog, anxiety, and concussion recovery, where the brain's filtering system is already working overtime.
