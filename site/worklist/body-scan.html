Benefits of Body Scan Meditation

For Anxiety

Body scans shift focus from racing thoughts to physical sensations, activating the parasympathetic nervous system and reducing the fight-or-flight response.

For Trauma

Gentle body awareness helps rebuild a sense of safety in the body. It supports nervous system regulation and can reduce hypervigilance over time.

For Sleep

Systematic relaxation of each body part signals to your brain that it is safe to rest. Many people use body scans as a sleep induction technique.
